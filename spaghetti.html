Classic Spaghetti Recipe 🍝

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb spaghetti pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • Salt, pepper, basil to taste
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, simmer for 15 mins.
  4. Mix sauce with drained spaghetti, top with parmesan.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your pasta feast! 🍝
