Classic Apple Pie Recipe 🍏

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter, chilled
  • 6-7 cups sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the pie crust and chill.
  2. Mix apples, sugar, cinnamon, and flour.
  3. Fill crust with apple mixture, cover with top crust.
  4. Brush with egg wash and bake at 375°F for 50 minutes.
  5. Cool before serving. Enjoy warm with ice cream! 🍎
