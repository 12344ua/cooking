Classic Mac & Cheese Recipe ğŸ

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the macaroni until tender. Drain.
  2. In the same pot, add butter, milk, and cheese.
  3. Mix until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add macaroni back in, stir well, and season to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy that cheesy goodness! ğŸ§€
